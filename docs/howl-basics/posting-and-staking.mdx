# Posting and Staking

   
   ### How to post
   
   1) Click on **Post** at the bottom of the left pane on the howl app
      
      ![image](https://github.com/bilalkhan360/howldocs/blob/main/static/img/howl%20post.png)
   
   2) type what you want to post in the blank space and click on **Post**.
      
      ![image](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bilalkhan360/howldocs/21bf7d8c3302d33a8528e0e8897ff748463bcf17/static/img/Howl%20post%202.png)
     
   3) Aprrove the keplr txn prompt
   
   ### How to stake
   
   1) Find a post on Howl social you want to stake your Howl to
      
      Note : you can stake to more than 1 post at a time
      
   2) click on the heart/like icon below the post 
      
      ![image](https://github.com/bilalkhan360/howldocs/blob/main/static/img/howl%20stake%20.png)
   
   3) type the amount of Howl you want to stake and click **Stake**. Then approve the txn request
    
      ![image](https://github.com/bilalkhan360/howldocs/blob/main/static/img/howl%20stake%20amount.png)
